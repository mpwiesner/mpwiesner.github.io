Saturday Afternoon Astronomy



Saturday Afternoon Astronomy (SAA) is a program at Purdue introducing concepts in astronomy to people of all ages. SAA will begin in September 2015. Purdue astronomers will introduce a concept in astronomy and take questions about it for the first half of the session. For the second half we will be doing a hands-on activity to learn more about the astronomy. Watch this space for more information!
