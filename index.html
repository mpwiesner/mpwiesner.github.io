Professor Matt Wiesner



I am an Assistant Professor of physics and engineering at Benedictine University in Lisle, Illinois. My research is in gravitational lensing, interacting galaxies and galaxy clusters. I am a member of the Large Synoptic Survey Telescope (LSST) Dark Energy Science Collaboration (DESC) and the Dark Energy Survey Gravitational Wave program. I am also interested in solar system astronomy, physics education research and the prevention and study of pseudoscience.
