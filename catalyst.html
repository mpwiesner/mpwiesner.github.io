The LSST Catalyst Fellowship Expansion Mentors summer program is a summer internship at Benedictine University where students will work on a research project with a Catalyst Fellow. Most of the work will be done at Benedictine University in Lisle.

This is a full-time internship for 10 weeks. Computing knowledge is helpful but not required. To learn more about the Catalyst Fellows, go to the Catalyst Fellows Page.

To apply for the internship please go to the application form
